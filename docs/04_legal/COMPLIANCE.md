# Compliance

## What this is
High-level privacy and content compliance notes.

## What it's for
Keeps the repository honest about data handling, third-party content, and ownership.

## How to create or update
Update this file when new data collection, embeds, or legal requirements are introduced.

## Current notes
- The site is personal and does not store user accounts or profile data.
- Contact interactions are currently local/demo-only in the UI.
- Third-party images and embeds must be reviewed for licensing and usage terms.
- Secrets and API keys must stay out of the repository.

## Best practices
- Document any future analytics, forms, or data retention changes here.
- Keep license and attribution notes close to the content that needs them.
